## 12 Content Gap Opportunities

### Phase 1 — Immediate (High Impact)

#### GAP 1: Validade e Renovacao da Prova ICAO
**URL:** `/validade-e-renovacao-prova-icao`
**Queries:** validade prova ICAO, renovacao ICAO, quanto tempo vale ICAO nivel 4, renovar proficiencia linguistica ANAC
**Why:** No competitor has a dedicated page. L4=3yr, L5=6yr, L6=lifetime with practical renewal guidance. Very common question across forums.
**Priority: HIGH** — Evergreen, feeds commercial funnel.

#### GAP 2: SDEA — O Que E o Santos Dumont English Assessment
**URL:** `/sdea-santos-dumont-english-assessment`
**Queries:** SDEA, Santos Dumont English Assessment, o que e SDEA, diferenca SDEA ICAO
**Why:** Official ANAC name. Many candidates search this term. Only ANAC's bureaucratic page ranks — opportunity for accessible content.
**Priority: HIGH** — Captures alternate keyword cluster.

#### GAP 3: Prova ICAO Reprovado — O Que Acontece Se Reprovar
**URL:** `/reprovado-na-prova-icao`
**Queries:** prova ICAO reprovado, o que acontece se reprovar, prazo para refazer SDEA, nivel 3 ICAO o que fazer
**Why:** High-anxiety topic. 60-day wait, Level 3 = not operational, cannot fly internationally. No single comprehensive resource.
**Priority: HIGH** — Emotionally charged, strong funnel to course sales.

#### GAP 4: Vocabulario de Aviacao em Ingles / Glossario ICAO
**URL:** `/vocabulario-aviacao-ingles`
**Queries:** vocabulario aviacao ingles, vocabulario ICAO, termos aviacao em ingles, glossario aviacao ICAO, fraseologia aeronautica
**Why:** ~400 standard phraseology words. Searchable glossary = massive long-tail SEO asset, earns backlinks.
**Priority: HIGH** — Massive long-tail potential, linkable authority asset.

### Phase 2 — Short Term (Funnel Builders)

#### GAP 5: Simulado Prova ICAO / Como Praticar
**URL:** `/simulado-prova-icao`
**Queries:** simulado prova ICAO, simulado ICAO online, prova ICAO modelo, treinar para prova ICAO
**Why:** Competitors (Vector, GearUp, ClearSky) dominate with free simulators. Content page + link to Easycao method as premium solution.
**Priority: HIGH** — Very high commercial intent.

#### GAP 6: Erros Comuns na Prova ICAO
**URL:** `/erros-comuns-prova-icao`
**Queries:** erros comuns prova ICAO, maiores erros ICAO, por que pilotos reprovam na ICAO
**Why:** inglesicao.com.br has a series but no comprehensive single resource. Anxiety-reducing, shareable content.
**Priority: MEDIUM-HIGH**

#### GAP 7: Como Agendar a Prova ICAO
**URL:** `/como-agendar-a-prova-icao`
**Queries:** como agendar prova ICAO, agendar SDEA, marcar prova ICAO, formulario SDEA
**Why:** Step-by-step process not covered in one place. Find entity → contact → fill SDEA form → bring documents → results in 30 business days.
**Priority: MEDIUM-HIGH**

#### GAP 8: Quem Precisa Fazer a Prova ICAO
**URL:** `/quem-precisa-fazer-prova-icao`
**Queries:** quando preciso fazer prova ICAO, piloto privado precisa ICAO, ICAO para voo domestico, ICAO obrigatoria
**Why:** Confusion about who needs it. Not mandatory for PLA, but airlines require it. Not needed for domestic-only PPR.
**Priority: MEDIUM-HIGH** — Top-of-funnel capture.

| Sep-Nov | RISING AGAIN | Second exam wave before year-end |
