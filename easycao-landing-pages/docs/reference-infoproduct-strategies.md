# Infoproduct Sales Page Strategies — Reference for Easycao /metodo

Research compiled March 2026. Source: Analysis of top Brazilian infoproduct creators and market practices.

---

## Optimal Sales Page Structure (Brazilian Market)

### Section Order (High-Converting)

1. **Hero (Above the Fold)** — Transformation headline + subheadline + primary CTA + hero media (VSL or instructor photo)
2. **Problem Identification** — "Voce ja tentou estudar sozinho e..."
3. **Agitation** — Consequences of not solving the problem
4. **Authority/Credibility** — Instructor bio, credentials, media
5. **Method/Solution Reveal** — What makes this approach different
6. **Social Proof Block #1** — Video testimonials (3-5 short clips)
7. **Course Content/Modules** — What's included (accordion or cards)
8. **Bonuses** — Always present, stacked with perceived value
9. **Social Proof Block #2** — Screenshots of results, numbers, certificates
10. **Pricing Section** — With anchoring (value stack vs actual price)
11. **Guarantee** — 7-day minimum, 30-day preferred
12. **FAQ** — 8-12 questions addressing objections
13. **Final CTA** — Urgency-driven closing
14. **Footer** — Legal, CNPJ, privacy (LGPD required)

### CTA Placement

- **Minimum 4-6 CTAs** distributed throughout
- First: Above the fold
- Second: After social proof #1
- Third: After course content
- Fourth: In/after pricing
- Fifth: After FAQ
- Sixth: Floating/sticky on mobile
- Brazilian pages tend to have **more CTAs than American pages** (some 8-10)

### Page Length

- R$297-R$997: 3,000-6,000 words
- R$997-R$2,997+: 5,000-10,000+ words, often with VSL
- **Long-form pages dominate** in Brazil — thoroughness is valued

## Copywriting Frameworks

### PAS (Problem-Agitation-Solution) — Best for Exam Prep

- **Problem**: "Voce estuda horas por dia mas na hora da prova nao lembra de nada?"
- **Agitation**: "Enquanto isso, seus colegas estao sendo aprovados..."
- **Solution**: "O Metodo [X] usa tecnicas que fazem voce reter 3x mais conteudo"

### Hybrid PAS + StoryBrand (Recommended for Easycao)

1. Open with PAS to hook emotionally
2. Transition to StoryBrand (instructor as guide, not authority)
3. Close with AIDA-style urgency

### Key Positioning: "Method" > "Course"

- "Curso" implies passive consumption
- "Metodo" implies a system that produces results
- **"NAO e um curso. E um METODO de aprovacao."**

## Conversion Optimization — Brazilian Specifics

### Social Proof That Works

- **Video testimonials** — 15-60 sec clips showing real results
- **WhatsApp screenshots** — Uniquely Brazilian, feel authentic
- **Precise numbers** — "12.847 alunos aprovados" > "milhares de alunos"
- **"Mural de aprovados"** — Gallery of approved students (standard for exam prep)
- **Instagram/social proof** — Screenshots of stories showing results
- **2-3 social proof locations** on the page, not just one section

### Urgency Techniques

- Countdown timers (still effective)
- Limited bonus availability
- Class/turma closing dates
- Price increase warnings
- "Lote" pricing (Lote 1, 2, 3)
- **Real deadlines > artificial scarcity** (Brazilian consumers are skeptical of fake scarcity)

### Pricing Presentation

- **Value stacking is essential:**
  ```
  Modulo Principal ................ R$1.997
  Bonus 1: Simulados ............. R$497
  Bonus 2: Grupo VIP ............. R$297
  VALOR TOTAL: R$2.791
  HOJE POR APENAS: R$497
  ou 12x de R$49,70
  ```
- **Always show "parcelado" prominently** — Brazilians think in monthly payments
- **PIX discount** (5-10%) — now standard and expected
- **Three-tier pricing** when applicable (Basic / Complete / VIP)

### Guarantee

- 7 days = legal minimum (Hotmart/Kiwify CDC)
- **30 days = sweet spot** — shows confidence
- "Garantia incondicional" performs well
- Risk reversal: "O risco e todo meu. Se voce nao gostar, devolvo cada centavo."
- Extended: "Se voce seguir o metodo e nao passar, devolvemos" (conditional but powerful)

### WhatsApp Integration (Non-Negotiable)

- Floating button for pre-sale questions (15-30% conversion lift)
- "Tire suas duvidas pelo WhatsApp" — functions as support AND sales
- WhatsApp remarketing for abandoned carts (with LGPD consent)

## Mobile Optimization (Critical)

- **70-80% of Brazilian infoproduct traffic is mobile** (higher than US/EU)
- Sticky CTA button at bottom of mobile screen
- WhatsApp floating button
- Accordion sections for modules/FAQ
- Minimum 48px button height, full-width on mobile
- Target under 3 seconds LCP
- Font minimum 16px on mobile
- Video: Use thumbnail with play button (avoid autoplay issues)

## Brazilian Audience Psychology

1. **Emotional connection > logical argument** — feel understood before seeing features
2. **Instructor personality matters more** — personal brand > course content
3. **Community/belonging is major driver** — "Faca parte da nossa comunidade de +10.000 alunos"
4. **FOMO is strong** — "Todo mundo ja esta usando esse metodo"
5. **Informal tone converts better** — "Voce" not "O senhor"
6. **Trust signals critical** — due to historical online fraud concerns
7. **Transformation narrative** — "rags to riches through knowledge" deeply embedded

## For Exam Prep Specifically

### Purchase Motivations

1. Career advancement/salary increase
2. Time pressure (upcoming exam date = natural urgency)
3. Previous failure — "Ja reprovou? Voce nao e o problema, seu metodo e"
4. Competitive pressure
5. Financial justification — "Quanto custa mais um ano sem passar?"
6. Family/social expectations
7. Fear of wasting time

### Instructor Authority (E-E-A-T for Sales)

1. **Personal results** — "Aprovado em 1o lugar"
2. **Student results** — names, photos, exam scores
3. **Years of experience** — "10 anos preparando alunos"
4. **Media appearances** — TV, podcasts
5. **Credentials** — positioned as "practitioner who teaches"
6. **Approval rate** — "87% dos nossos alunos sao aprovados"
7. **Association with institutions** — examiner, question writer

**Key insight**: "Professor que ja passou" > "Professor com PhD" — practical authority trumps academic

### Success Rate as Conversion Driver

- Specific: "87,3% de aprovacao" > "quase 90%"
- Comparison: "3x a media nacional"
- Contextualized: "87% dos alunos que completaram o metodo"
- Visual: progress bar or infographic
- Updated: "Dados atualizados em [month/year]"

## Technical SEO for Sales Pages

### Schema Markup (Recommended)

- **Course schema** with instructor, provider, offers, aggregate rating
- **FAQPage schema** for FAQ section
- **BreadcrumbList** for navigation
- **VideoObject** if public video present

### Index Strategy

- **Index** the /metodo page if targeting commercial keywords AND has educational content
- Consider separate indexed "product page" (SEO) + noindexed "launch page" (campaigns)

### Performance Budget

- Total page weight: Under 1.5MB initial (without video)
- TTI: Under 3.5 seconds on 4G
- FCP: Under 1.5 seconds
- Use video facade pattern (static thumbnail → load player on click)
