Scroll Reveal Pattern

Uses useInView(0.1) hook with IntersectionObserver. Elements start at opacity-0 translate-y-5 and transition to opacity-100 translate-y-0. Staggered delays: transitionDelay: ${i * 100-150}ms. Fires once — observer disconnects after first intersection.
